About Me

My name is Yannik Quellmalz, I am 21 years old and I am currently working as a "Service Technician" at ST Sportservice GmbH in Leipzig. In September 2023, I successfully completed my studies in media informatics and thus attained the academic degree of "Bachelor of Engineering".

More About Me

I have been interested in computer science, graphic design and video editing since my school days. That's why I decided to study "media informatics" dual at the Dresden University of Cooperative Education (Berufsakademie Sachsen - Standort Dresden) after graduating from high school in 2020. My training partner was ST Sportservice GmbH in Leipzig.

After I finished my studies in September 2023, I was taken on by my practice partner ST Sportservice GmbH and have been working and living in Leipzig ever since.

In my free time I like to do sports. I actively go to a gym myself, but I also like to play team sports. So I played soccer for a long time before I devoted myself to basketball.
